Sample 3: Chinese Culture Game for Travelers

In an effort to win a U.S. government contract, some friends and I created a prototype module of a game teaching some finer points of Chinese culture. The game puts the learner in various business and social situations on a trip to China. As you will see, much of the knowledge imparted comes via feedback statements; it's an inversion of the usual "Tell and Test" method commonly seen in e-learning. I co-designed the format and wrote the questions and feedback statements.

Because the game was built with the now-defunct Adobe Flash, I cannot share the game itself, but I am able to share a walkthrough video recorded before Flash was discontinued.
